Original microinteraction

Triggers

  • The microinteraction is triggered when you hover the mouse over the white box containing the Account Settings label.

Rules / Feedback

  • The pointer icon instantly turns to the standard hand icon for clickable items.
  • The box containing the Account Settings label instantly moves up about 5-10 pixels.
  • The feedback is the same as the rules, as nothing happens in the background.

Loops/Modes

  • There are no loops or modes for this microinteraction.

New microinteraction

Triggers

  • The microinteraction is triggered when you hover the mouse over the white box containing the Account Settings label.

Rules/Feedback

  • The pointer icon instantly turns to the standard hand icon for clickable items.
  • A border that matches the company's purple color appears around the clickable box, growing from 1 pixel to 5 pixels in width within the .4 second duration time of the microinteraction.
  • The little gray sparkle images shaped like plus signs, situated to the top right of the cogs, will not be static. They will start off completely transparent, and four small 2 pixel width lines will appear and expand out from a center point, quickly disappearing by shortening in length until it has a length of 0.
  • For the rule above, this motion will apply to both sparkles. The first sparkle that is higher up will start animating immediately on hover, and the second sparkle that is lower down will start animating .2 seconds after the first.
  • The two cogs will spin immediately on hover, the yellow cog rotating 180 degrees clockwise, and the purple cog will rotate 180 degrees counter-clockwise. The rotation speed will start at maximum speed and slow down as the animation plays through the full .4 second length.
  • The feedback is the same as the rules, as nothing happens in the background.

Loops/Modes

  • There are no loops or modes for this microinteraction.
